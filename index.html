About Me

I'm a research scientist specializing in large language models (LLMs), numerical analysis, and GPU‑accelerated AI. My work spans from solving hard mathematical problems—numerical PDEs and Bayesian inverse problems—to building scalable LLM systems.

I design retrieval‑augmented pipelines, multi‑tool agents, and distributed training workflows (DDP, DeepSpeed, FSDP) that leverage custom GPU kernels (CUDA, NCCL) for efficient training and inference. I enjoy bridging rigorous mathematics with modern AI, turning theory into robust software and agentic workflows that orchestrate multiple tools to deliver reliable results.
